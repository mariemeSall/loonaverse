WELCOME TO THE LOONNAVERSE

On this web site, you can navigate trough the members and subunits infromations but also listen to their music on the album tab.

Loona is 12members korean girl group. They have a very interesting and intricate lore.

From the korean name 이달의 소녀, Loona has two meanings: Girl of the month and Girl of the moon.

GIRL OF THE MONTH

At the beginning of Loona, each month a new member or subunit was introduced. Every member was introduce with a solo album single and had their own music video

GIRL OF THE MOON

Loona has a strong connexion with the moon's phases. At the beginning of all music video, you can see the moon changing.
